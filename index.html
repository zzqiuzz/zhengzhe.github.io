  •   • 2019/2-2019/5:   Quantize Generative Adversarial Networks.

    •  We propose a method to simultaneously quantize weights and activations in GANs, we use Frchet Inception Distance score to evaluate generated images of quantized GANs. Sensitivity analysis shows that weights are more sensitive than activation in quantization process. Motivated by this phenomenon,extensive experiments are conducted on Mnist and Celeb-A datasets. Results show that we can compress GANs by up to 4x and still achieve even higher performance than the original GANs.
